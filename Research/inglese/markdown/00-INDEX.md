## Abstract

WhyCremisi is the first native AI system for professional music production that does not merely *suggest* changes — it **executes** them, controlling in real time the entire production chain: DAW, third-party plugins, automations, effects and mix. The agent operates with persistent memory, evolutionary personality and contextual knowledge of the current session.
