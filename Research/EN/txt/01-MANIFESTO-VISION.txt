## 1. The Problem We Solve

Professional music production is a system of extraordinary complexity. An experienced sound engineer simultaneously manages dozens of tracks, chains of processors (EQ, compressors, limiters, reverbs), parametric automations across hundreds of values, and real-time creative decisions.

Existing AI tools offer suggestions. They stop there.

WhyCremisi does not stop at the suggestion. It acts.

---

## 2. The Vision

`
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│                                                         │
│   An AI agent that lives inside the DAW,                │
│   knows every installed plugin,                         │
│   listens to every audio signal in real time,           │
│   remembers every previous session,                     │
│   and intervenes — with the producer's permission —     │
│   on any parameter of any instrument.                   │
│                                                         │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
`

### Control Levels

`
  LEVEL 4  Creative    — Suggests arrangements, harmonic variations
  LEVEL 3  Mix         — Controls EQ, compressors, panning, gain
  LEVEL 2  Plugins     — FabFilter, iZotope, Waves, UAD parameters
  LEVEL 1  DAW         — Transport, routing, automations, timeline
`

---

## 3. Core Principles

- Non-invasiveness by design — every action requires explicit consent
- Total transparency — every action logged, explained, reversible
- Contextual learning — learns the producer's style over time
- Universal instrument support — no plugin excluded

---

| Tool | Type | Limitations |
